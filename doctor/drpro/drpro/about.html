About Me

Mark Yurek, MD, specializes in family medicine and is board-certified in family medicine by the American Board of Family Medicine. He practices at UPMC Primary Care Chambersburg and is affiliated with UPMC Harrisburg, UPMC West Shore, and UPMC Community Osteopathic. Dr. Yurek earned his medical degree at Universidad Central del Este. He completed his residencies at UPMC Harrisburg, UPMC Community Osteopathic.
